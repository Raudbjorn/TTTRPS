# UX Requirements

## 1. NPC Management (Sidebar)
**Requirement**: Each campaign view must have a dedicated sidebar area displaying NPCs relevant to that campaign.
**Details**:
- Valid for "Each Campaign".
- persistent place for NPCs.
- Quick access to view/edit or drag-into-scene (implied availability).

## 2. Campaign Session Management
**Requirement**: Campaigns must organize sessions into three distinct states:
- **Past**: Completed sessions with logs/notes.
- **Current**: The active or next upcoming session.
- **Planned**: Future session ideas or outlines.
**Details**:
- Selectable list for navigation.
- Clear visual distinction between states.

## 3. Personality Manager
**Requirement**: Accessibility and configurability of generic or specific personalities.
**Details**:
- "Select a character from a specific source document".
- Configurable interface (likely a modal or dedicated settings page).

## 4. Voice & Audio Controls
**Requirement**: Integrated audio controls for generated text.
**Details**:
- "Play" button next to all Character/DM output text blocks.
- Only visible/active if voice is configured.

## 5. Live Transcription
**Requirement**: Control over real-time speech-to-text.
**Details**:
- Toggle (Enable/Disable) for "Live" transcription.
- Slick UI element (switch/button).

## 6. General UX/UI
**Requirement**: "Slick, usable, beautiful experience".
**Details**:
- Modern aesthetics.
- Responsive layout.
- High usability for DMs active in a session.
